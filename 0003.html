Here are some recent updates to in5:


  • The new Accessibility widget*** lets you configure custom tags, ARIA roles, skip links, and screen reader visibility for maximum 508 compliance.
  • The Fixed Position Element** widget lets you “pin” items like menus to the browser window so that they stay in place as you scroll.
  • You can now create a Parallax Background** image on any of your pages.
  • You can now export Alternate Layouts as Responsive Layouts and they will automatically change in your browser based on the window size or orientation.**
  • You can now easily create One-Page Scrolling Websites using the Easy Export Wizard (or the Borderless Scrolling Page Format in the full export).
  • The Easy Export Wizard lets you export all different types of content (websites, digital magazines, presentations, etc) without having to figure out which options to use.
  • When you’re done exporting, check out the What do I do now? button.
  • The Viewer Display* now lets you brand your control frame with a logo and custom color.


* Available with Pro plans or higher.
** Available with Gold Plans or higher.

*** Available with Elite and Enterprise plans.
