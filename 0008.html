You can extend the interactive capability of InDesign using in5 > Interactive Widgets, which includes:

  • Audio
  • Video
  • Hyperlinks
  • Scrollable Frame
  • Slideshow
  • Pan & Zoom
  • Image Sequence
  • Web Content (for HTML files)
  • 3D Flip Card*
  • Form Element Data*
  • Mobile Article Explorer*
  • Fixed Position Element**
  • Parallax Background**


*Requires a Pro plan (or higher).

** Available with Gold Plans or higher.
